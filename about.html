About U-City Grill

University City Grill was established in 1989 by the Lee family. It started as a small fast food restaurant serving home-made Korean dishes. Since its founding, U City Grill has grown to become a local favorite. To this day, it still retains the small, diner style that welcomes customers with its home feel and food.
